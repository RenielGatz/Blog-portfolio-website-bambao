Demo-Teaching

Reflection 4

On day 5 of demo teaching (week 10) in Home Economics, I learned that preparation is crucial to becoming an effective facilitator who can handle the unique behaviors of each learner. A positive classroom environment can greatly impact the learning process and keep students motivated. Lesson plans help us prepare to teach a particular topic.

According to Prof. Alvadaro, analyzing the topic and the flow of the lesson plan should be the basis of the learning process. When choosing a topic, Prof. Florendo advised us to use the SMART method, which emphasizes being specific. When I first made a detailed lesson plan, I was overwhelmed because it was almost 15 pages long for a 45-minute class. But now I understand that choosing a specific topic is significant when taking a demo teaching. Demo teaching prepares me to be a great facilitator in classroom it should be always make it simple to method of teaching with interactive with its learner that's why, create a plan and analyzing the learner are the essential part of learning process.

In conclusion, we should always utilize the SMART method when creating a lesson plan and analyze our audience or learners to carry out an effective lecture in the learning environment.
